Beginners & First Time

Things to Bring

  • Waterproof ski jacket, waterproof Ski pants, thermal pants and shirt, a mid layer long sleeve shirt and long pants, thick socks, ski gloves, and a can do attitude.
  • Shoes or boots with traction
  • Lip balm to protect from dry lips
  • Suncreen
  • Goggles (we reccomend sunglasses for kids)
  • Neck warmer to keep your neck, mouth and nose warm
  • Helmet is essential while skiing but bring a beanie for taking breaks

Safe Slopes

If you are scared of the slopes there is no need to worry. Here at Snowy mountain ski resort have taught millions of people how to ski and snowboard. With ski instructors on hand, easy breezy begginer slopes, and a magic carpet ski lifts to help you to the top, we will have you shredding like the best in no time. For those who just want to relax for the morning/afternoon or the whole day, The toboggan track is perfect for racing your friends without any equipment at all.
