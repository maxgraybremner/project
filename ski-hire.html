Equipment Hire & Shop

Equipment Hire

OH NO! You forgot your gloves at home! No worries, the Snowy Mountain rental hut has you covered. Everything from skis, snowboards, boots, gloves, jackets and more can be hired right here. For all those powder addicts, we offer extra wide powder skis to help you float over the fresh snow. All snowboards are waxed by our staff to ensure your boardslide is extra silky and if you find your boots don't fit quite right, we will replace them as quick as we can to get you back on the chairlift.

Ski Shop

They don't make them like they used to! Unfortunately those hand-me-down pants with the hole is side just don't keep the cold out like before. So try on a new pair at the Snow Shop. Here we are more than happy to help you find a new pair of goggles, a fresh beanie, or a whole new outfit for the season. Our shop assistants can get you ready for the coldest of days.
