Snowy Mountain Ski Field

Our Purpose

Here at the Snowy Mountain Ski Field our team wants to make sure you have the best experience possible. Our warm and welcoming ski instructors are ready to instruct even the most novice of snow riders, and for the more extreme powder enthusiasts, you can choose between our custom-built terrain park or darting in and out of the trees in the neighbouring Yowie Forest.

Our history

Located within the stunning Kosciuszko National Park region in New South Wales, Snowy Mountain Ski Field has been a rite of passage for ski enthusiasts since 1967. It is one of four major ski fields in the region. Since our founding, it has been a favourite ski destinatino for families and snow veterans alike.
